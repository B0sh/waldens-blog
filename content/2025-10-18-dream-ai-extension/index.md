My dream AI browser extension is a decontenting of advertising and marketing psychology tricks.

- First, and most important I *never* want to see a percentage off sign. 8% off? 90% off? I don't care. Never show me the "real price"/MSRP. "sales" do not exist. What price is it right now?
    - I'd be ok to be shown an [average product price](https://camelcamelcamel.com/) from history and how it compares to now.
- Furthermore, remove all countdown timers for purchases.
- Don't show me product stock. Just stop me if I try to buy and there's no stock left.
- Show the actual product amount. It's not 3 toothbrushes + 1 FREE EXTRA. How about just 4 toothbrushes please.
- Calcuate all plan options and display a final total price.
    - 3 months for 10$, and the after that it's 60$ a month? Show me its `$10*3 + $60*9 = $570` for the first year, don't show me the gradient pricing.

This definitely falls into the "reality altering" aspect of AI. If it was just text I feel like this could be done now, but to really do the concept well it would have to extend to product images/videos and source code. Good luck with that...
